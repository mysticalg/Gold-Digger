How to Play Gold Digger

  • This map is 200x1000 blocks; horizontal movement wraps around seamlessly.
  • Start on the surface lane, move left/right to pick your spot, then dig through the first grass layer.
  • Digging sideways can destabilize columns and make blocks fall.
  • Rock needs Rock Drill. Metal/Granite are best handled with bombs.
  • Collect treasure, relics, crystals, and deep mineral veins (gold, diamonds, rubies, emeralds, sapphires, gemstones) for money + XP.
  • Press Space to use a bomb.
  • Each successful dig consumes 1 stamina. You cannot dig with zero stamina.
  • Return to the 🛖 cottage and resting starts automatically; stamina refills point-by-point over 10 seconds.
  • Stand at the 🛒 shop surface tile to buy upgrades (including Well Unlock and NPC Digger).
  • Collect water at the 🪣 well (next to the shop) and splash it on lava to convert lava into granite.
  • Lava pockets can leak if containment breaks; lava is unpassable and dangerous.
  • Press M to toggle sound effects.
  • Press B to toggle build mode, E to place selected tile, and C to cycle placeable inventory tiles.
  • Torches and struts can be bought in the shop and placed underground to light caverns and support excavations.
  • Use Cheat Mode for testing: unlocks upgrades, fast dig, infinite money, stamina, bombs, and water.
  • Fog-of-war reveals only a smooth 5-block sight radius; distant/unexplored cells fade to black.
  • Press Z to toggle a fixed 20x20 zoomed play-area view (now default on start).
  • If you are fully trapped by unbreakable rock/metal/granite with no bombs left, it is game over.
