How to Play Gold Digger

  • Start at the top and dig through sand blocks.
  • Digging sideways can make loose sand above fall down.
  • Rock blocks need Rock Drill upgrade.
  • Metal and Granite require bombs.
  • Collect treasure to earn money, XP, and unlock tougher sites.
  • Buy tools to dig faster, create wider tunnels, and carry more bombs.
